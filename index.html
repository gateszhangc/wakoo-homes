Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started on Wakoo.

Is Wakoo free to use? ▼
Yes, Wakoo offers a full-featured free tier that includes profile creation, messaging, and joining group chats. Premium subscriptions unlock additional features like unlimited likes, advanced filters, and ad-free browsing.
How does Wakoo verify profiles? ▼
We use a multi-step verification process that includes email confirmation, phone number validation, and optional photo-based verification. Verified profiles are clearly marked so you know who you are talking to is genuine.
Can I delete my account at any time? ▼
Absolutely. You can delete your account and all associated data directly from your settings page at any time. We believe you should always have full control over your presence on Wakoo.
What are voice chat rooms? ▼
Voice chat rooms are real-time audio spaces where multiple people can join and talk together. They are organized by topics and interests, making it easy to find conversations you will enjoy. No video required unless you choose to share.
How does matchmaking work on Wakoo? ▼
Our matchmaking engine considers your stated preferences, your activity patterns, the interests you engage with, and feedback from your interactions. It improves over time as you use the platform, surfacing people who are genuinely compatible with you.
